World Overview

The World of Elucidate

A world fractured between undivine order and formless chaos, where five orphaned souls unknowingly shaped the war entirely.

Lumen & The Church

The Church of Lumen (Also known as "Lumenaries") built its doctrine upon the genuine cosmic principle of order and light — then weaponized it for control. Behind cathedral walls and holy doctrine, they conducted experiments on orphan children, forging Vindication Lumen — soldiers who could endure the Zones of Terrors without losing their minds.

Caligo & The Cult

Caligo the Formless predates the Church by centuries. It does not desire worship. It exists the way weather exists — without intention, but with terrible consequence. The Cult of Caligo rose from the Church's discarded experiments: children expelled as defects, gathered under Caligo's ambient influence on a hidden island.

Subject I-3 & The Orphanage

All five playable characters trace their origin to the same orphanage. Experiment Subject I-3 — designated Lucidus, born Ignis — is the most stable result. He does not know this. The war he fights for is the system that made him. Every mission brings him closer to a truth he was trained never to find.

In the beginning, the world existed in fragile balance between Lumen and Caligo — order and chaos, light and formless darkness. Neither was wholly good nor evil. They were two poles of existence. When balance held, the world prospered. When it was broken, the world decayed. Both the Church and the Cult are corrupt versions of a genuine cosmic principle. The war between them is not good versus evil — it is two forms of human corruption fighting over the corpse of something that was once true.

Artifacts & Relics

Highlighted Items

The relics that connect the Church's agenda, the Cult's ritual, and the truth neither side wants spoken.

Ancient Paper
Rare · Document

Used to craft the "Book of Enlightenment". Proof that the Church and Cult's methodologies share more than either would admit — the first written hint that this war was staged.

Caligo
Mystic · Cosmic Entity

The Formless One. Predates the Church by centuries. Manifests through the Fragments as physical concentrations of its ambient influence — neither evil nor benevolent. Simply present, like weather.

Fragment of Caligo — Eye Form
Legendary · Fragment

The largest Fragment, held by the Cult Leader on Cultist Island. The Church wants to weaponize it. The Cult guards it as sacred. Pluvia warns it will cost whoever carries it something — that is still the best outcome she can see.

Flame Fragment
Legendary · Fragment

One of the three Fragments scattered across the world. Its warmth mirrors Ignis — the name they suppressed, the fire they tried to make obedient. In Path B, it becomes the instrument of the Church's end.

Factions

The Two Powers

Two corrupted expressions of a genuine cosmic truth, locked in a war that was never about what they claim it is.

Church of Lumen
"Diplomats reason. You conclude."

Ancient order founded on the genuine cosmic principle of Lumen — order, light, structure. Corrupted by human ambition into a theocratic military machine that conducts underground experiments on orphan children, building soldiers capable of enduring the Zones of Terrors. Their goal: control all three Fragments of Caligo to achieve absolute dominance over darkness.

Cult of Caligo
"It was not a coincedence. Nothing was."

Born from the Church's discarded experiments — children expelled as defects, survivors who found each other under Caligo's ambient influence. They guard the largest Fragment as sacred, believing Caligo is misunderstood rather than evil. They are not wrong about the Church. They are simply doing it badly, and alone, and they have been told their entire lives that they are nothing.

MS Mind State

The Mind State System

Every zone, every encounter, every revelation costs something. The Mind State meter measures psychological stability — and when it reaches 100, the player doesn't lose the game. They lose themselves. Zones of Terrors accelerate its rise. Blue Gems slow the collapse. The Shaman halves the rate entirely — IF YOU SAVE HER IN TIME

0–49 — Stable : World as it is. Horrors are shadows.
50–74 — Strained : Proportions shift. NPCs react differently.
75–99 — Critical : Choices locked. Manifestations appear.
100 — Path C : Identity collapses. Caligo fills the void.
